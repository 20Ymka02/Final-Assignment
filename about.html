Our History and Coaches

The heart of our youth school

Our school unites girls of all ages around a love of sports. We don't just teach how to work with a ribbon, hoop, ball, or clubs - we educate leaders who are ready for any life challenges.

The coaching staff of the Youth Sports School consists of experienced mentors who know how to find a balance between discipline and support for the child.
